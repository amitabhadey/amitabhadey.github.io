## Academic Background
<span id="academic-background"></span>

I completed my **M.Sc. in Computer Science** at the **University of North Carolina at Greensboro (UNCG)**, where I worked as a graduate researcher in the **IFFS-ML Lab** under **Dr. Shan Suthaharan**.  
My thesis ‚Äî **LDEB: Label Digitization with Emotion Binarization** ‚Äî proposed a structured encoding approach for **emotion recognition in conversational dialogues**, enabling more stable learning in label-sparse environments.

My undergraduate thesis ‚Äî **Fake News Pattern Recognition using Linguistic Analysis** ‚Äî explored how linguistic cues can reveal author bias and deceptive intent in political social media discourse.
